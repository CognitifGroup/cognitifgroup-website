Charting the terrain of meaning, at population scale.

Cognitif Group is an enterprise software company building the Semantic Bathymetry™ Platform — a measurement discipline for the latent structure of language, delivered through our engine, Petalyx™.

What we do

Measurement, not modelling.

Large language models organise meaning into a landscape — some regions are sharply defined, where a population converges on a single association; others are broad and shallow, where meaning stays open and contested. Cognitif Group builds the instruments to chart that landscape at scale, so organisations can see where meaning is settled, where it is contested, and how that shifts over time.

The discipline

Semantic Bathymetry™

The measurement approach itself: treating a population's associative structure as a terrain with peaks of consensus and basins of ambiguity, and charting it the way a hydrographer charts a seabed.

The engine

Petalyx™

The instrument that takes the readings. A structured, six-axis measurement engine whose output is rendered as a rosette — one petal per axis.

The safeguard

Independent governance

Aggregate data is held by Semba AI, a separate company limited by guarantee, under its own board and ethics oversight — kept apart from the commercial entity by design.

Why bathymetry

Some meaning is a mountain. Some is open water.

A hydrographic chart doesn't just mark that water is present — it marks how deep, how steep, how the seabed changes shape. Cognitif Group's platform applies the same discipline to language: identifying the attractors where a population's associations converge with low variance, and the diffuse regions where interpretation stays wide open. That distinction is the measurement.
