This statement covers two related things: the lawful basis on which we process personal data, and the legitimate use of our platform and services.

Lawful basis. Where we rely on legitimate interests as our lawful basis under UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f), we have assessed that our interest in operating and improving our services is not overridden by the interests or fundamental rights of the individuals concerned. Where we process personal data under a contract, or with consent, or to meet a legal obligation, we rely on the corresponding basis under Article 6 instead, and will tell you which applies in context.
