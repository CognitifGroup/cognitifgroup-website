Governance

Cognitif Group and Semba AI are deliberately separate.

Cognitif Group Limited holds the commercial platform and the interpretive methodology. Semba AI, a company limited by guarantee, holds only the aggregate coordinate data produced by that platform, and acts as an independent custodian of it — governed by its own board rather than by Cognitif Group.
